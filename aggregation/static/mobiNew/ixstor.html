Hosted solutions.

Our hosted solutions provide irrevierable securty and encryption to your data. Simply put- we cannot decrypt the data that you have stored on our sever, no matter how hard we try. How is this possible? We do not know the keys for decrypting your data or the data transmissions between devices. Furthurmore, each session as unique keys that are generated to keep the data secure and encrypt the data on the drive while at rest. In addition to this, we leveralge full disk encryption on our servers, as well as encrypted back-up solutions. We like to desribe our hosted soltuions as a safehouse for your data. Your data lives in your storage area, and is toatally separate from everyone else's data.


Value Soltuions.-- Needs a better name!

A wonderful feature that iXStor implements is that it can be installed anywhere that an organization wants. This allows for extreme flexibily in managing devices and implementing a rollout. Transfering ixstor is simple as well! It relies on a database that is built when ixstor is first installed and configured. iXStor has the ability to encrypt data at rest. While this may be useful to most organizations, MobiWiSD realized that it is sometimes a feature that may not be widely accepted, so we do not enable it by default. Some orgnization leverage Active Directory and network shares to access data within an organization, so encrypting this data would make it inaccessible from within the oranizations network through the network shares and only available through a MobiWiSD application. This may not be practical for all. Fortuanlly, iXStor comes with a strong managment tool- MobiSage, which allows administrators to configure iXStor for their installations. iXStor does provide strong in transit securty and leverages both Public Key and Symmetric encryption algorithms that ensure that your data is never compromised.
