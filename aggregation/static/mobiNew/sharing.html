Simple sharing.

Share documents with ease.

Collaborating with electronic documents in an enterprise environment is difficult and presents various challenges to corporate security infrastructure.

With the introduction of mobile computing and an increase in employee mobility, collaboration has become increasingly difficult. Sending e-mail attachments presents a security risk and leads to revisions quickly getting lost, and forgotten. E-mail presents an insecure medium to collaborate across for it is vulnerable to a variety of attacks that could potentially leave sensitive documents exposed, or stolen. Other solutions attempt to solve the collaboration problem, but think of security after the fact. Collaboration should be secure, simple, flexible and should integrate into existing systems with ease.

MobiWiSD solutions for the enterprise implement secure sharing insofar that all of the collaboration is controlled and monitored by the document owner.

Our system enables sharing based on the internal company address book(s) and users who leverage our platform on their devices. This doesn't mean that you can only share within our platform, it simply means that sharing to mobile devices and controlling access and auditing access to documents has become much easier. Extending sharing to those outside of your organization can be easily done, granted the document owner allows it.
