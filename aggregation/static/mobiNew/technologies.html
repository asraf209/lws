Technologies.

At the core of all MobiWiSD solutions is iXStor, a technology developed to leverage our patent pending technology and provide security, effiiency and scalability for computing over 3G-4G networks. All of our solutions focus on Security, Efficiency, and Scalability for the enterprise.

Security

Manage data and devices in your cloud and guarantee security.

Data is continually encrypted with new and sophisticated encryption schemas making all types of attacks nearly impossible while ensuring confidentiality, integrity and availability of the data in transit.

Respond instantly to a lost or stolen device by disconnecting it from iXStor. Once the device is disconnected, data can no longer be accessed from that device.

Data is only stored where you want it. Point iXStor to your data locations and leverage MobiSafe to manage your mobile devices, data location, and backend integration. data is never stored locally on a mobile device.

Authenticate the user and device in seconds. iXStor knows which user is associated with which device, allowing organizations to manage both the user and the device.

Efficiency

Current mobile workforce solutions require high overhead VPN applications and appliances. iXstor eliminates the need to leverage VPN appliances and saves your company bandwidth at the same time.

Data is available to the user in a single touch. As long as the device belongs to that user, the data can be instantly available.

Sharing of data is simple. Company wide address books and permissions are easily set up by leveraging iXStor technologies and managing information with MobiSafe.

Scalability

Scale with your mobile footprint. iXstor allows for organizations to manage their infrastructure costs at their own pace. Add more storage, move your could, do whatever you'd like. iXStor will always be there.

Connect more devices seamlessly without having to worry about licensing fees or hardware requirements. iXstor will continue to accept incoming connections so long as there is a power and an internet connection.
