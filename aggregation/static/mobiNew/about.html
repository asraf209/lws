Our vision is to enter markets where data security is imperative to an organization's mission. These industries include; financial services, healthcare, and automotive. Additionally, our technology may find a home in many service based and manufacturing industries. Our framework is unique and flexible enough to cater to a diverse portfolio of industries. We strive to push the limits and test the capabilities of mobile devices.

MobiWiSD is a developer of mobile device management, deployment, and authentication solutions. We take mobile device management to a new level. Mobile device management solutions are currently limited to simply wiping the device, or gathering information about the device usage- forcing organizations to put solutions together from multiple vendors sources. While there is nothing wrong with this approach, fragmentation issues are still prevalent. Fragmentation of services can lead to increased costs stemming from IT needing to support and troubleshoot multiple services at any given moment. MobiWiSD solutions are designed to solve this problem with a standardized framework for data security that allows for administrators to control device operations, file sharing, user authentication and more with an increased level of granularity.

Currently, MobiWiSD is looking for investors and interested partners to join us on our mission of making data security implementation easy for policy writers and system administrators.
